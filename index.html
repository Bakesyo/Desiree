Biography

Desiree Austin Holliday is a distinguished civil rights attorney known for defending individual rights and advocating for justice. Born in Atlanta, Georgia—the youngest of four—she was inspired by her mother, Rev. Dr. Sharon G. Austin, the first female pastor at the historic Ebenezer Baptist Church. After relocating to Florida, she earned her bachelor’s degree in government from Berry College in 2014 and her law degree from St. Louis University School of Law in 2018.

During law school, Desiree actively engaged in community outreach, teaching law classes at local jails and juvenile detention centers. Initially practicing criminal defense, she soon expanded her focus to include civil litigation and civil rights law. As the supervising attorney at Ben Crump Law’s Las Vegas office, she leads a team of paralegals and manages a significant caseload with precision and compassion.
